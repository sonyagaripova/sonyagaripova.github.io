Hi! I am a geologist and geospatial analyst studying recent geodynamics in Geological Institute RAS.
I focus on active faults of Alpine-Himalayan (North Asia) and Circum-Pacific (Kamchatka) belts. After graduation from Geological faculty I started to work more with GIS. By now, I've been professionally engaged in geoscience for more than 4 years.

Mostly, I work with remote sensing to locate active faults and define their kinematics as it provides geodynamic reconstructions. I use satellite and UAV surveys data to conduct image classification, spatial analysis and develop 3D digital terrain models. To strengthen surface investigations, I conduct python-based geological modelling and use R and python for statistics.

Also, I am very much fascinated with mapping and maps design, and I always develop my GIS skills.
