Hi! I am a geologist and geospatial analyst studying paleoseismology and recent geodynamics in Geological Institute RAS in Moscow.

Mostly, I work with remote sensing to locate active faults and define their kinematics to reconstruct strain stress of the study area. I use satellite and UAV surveys data to conduct spatial analysis and develop 3D digital terrain models. Also, to strengthen surface investigations, I conduct python-based geological modelling to visualize 3D structures and formations.
